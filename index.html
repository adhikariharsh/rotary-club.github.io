our work

What we do?

Rotary members believe we have a shared responsibility to take action on our world's most persistent issue. Our 5,000+ members work together to achieve the best result to fight the problem that needy people are facing.

  • Supporting education
  • Providing clean water
  • Saving mothers and children
  • Promoting peace
  • Fighting against disease
  • Growing local economies
